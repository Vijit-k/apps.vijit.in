Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

1. Data Transparency & Collection

To provide our service, we collect and process the following information:

  • User Identity: We use the Chrome Identity API to access your Google Email to manage and sync your credit balance.
  • Unique Device ID: A randomly generated identifier is stored locally to prevent account sharing and assist in troubleshooting.
  • Generation Prompts: Text prompts entered into the extension are logged to facilitate AI generation.

2. Third-Party Data Processing

Text prompts are transmitted to Replicate to generate video content. We do not transmit your email address or personal identity to third-party AI providers.

3. Google API "Limited Use" Disclosure

This app adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Your data is never sold to third parties or used for advertising.

4. Refund & Credits Policy

We offer a 7-Day Risk-Free Refund Policy. If you have not utilized any of your purchased credits, you may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase by emailing support. Once a generation is attempted and a credit is deducted, that specific transaction becomes non-refundable due to the GPU costs incurred. Purchased credits do not expire and will remain in your account as long as the service is operational.
