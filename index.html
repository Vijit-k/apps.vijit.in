Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

AI Video Magic is committed to transparency and data security. Our extension is built to respect your privacy while providing a seamless AI experience.

1. Information Disclosure

To facilitate credit management, we access your Google Email via Chrome Identity. We also generate an Anonymous Device ID to protect against account abuse. Your email is never shared with third parties.

2. AI Generation Data

Your prompts are sent to our processing partner (Replicate) to generate video files. No personally identifiable information is attached to these prompts.

3. Google API Limited Use

AI Video Magic's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
